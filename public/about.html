About SiteSentinel

SiteSentinel is a practical website security and quality analysis platform designed for engineers and security teams. It focuses on delivering fast, auditable results and clear remediation steps so teams can reduce risk quickly.

Mission

Make security auditing accessible and actionable: provide deterministic checks that are easy to verify and concise guidance that helps teams prioritize and remediate issues.

What I built

  • Modular scanner with 60+ checks across Security, DNS, Performance, SEO, Accessibility and Safety.
  • Parallelized analysis pipeline for fast scan times and consistent scoring.
  • Clear, exportable reports and an extensible checks framework under src/checks/.
  • Optional AI layer for synthesized insights and prioritized remediation (kept advisory and separate from deterministic scoring).

Tech stack

Backend: Node.js, Express, Axios for HTTP, Cheerio for HTML parsing. Frontend: lightweight responsive dashboard with plain JS and modular components.
